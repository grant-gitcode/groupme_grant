How to set up the extension

To set up the extension, you must sign in with GroupMe in the pop up which appears when you first download the extension. At that point, you will stay signed in during and between browser sessions. If you remove the extension and close your current browser session, you will have to sign in again upon downloading the extension again.

How to use the extension

This extension allows the user to easily share links on GroupMe. Press Alt+Shift+G, or click the extension icon, when on a webpage which you wish to share. In the pop up window, type the name of the group to which you wish to share the link, and enter an optional message to share with the link. Hit enter when you're ready, and the link will be shared! It's that simple!
